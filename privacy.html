PediaCalc Pro Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 13, 2026

Overview

PediaCalc Pro is a pediatric emergency support calculator. The app is designed to work without a visible login, backend account, cloud sync, or export flow. Calculations, favorites, and recents are stored locally on the user’s device.

Information the app stores

PediaCalc Pro may store app preferences, favorite scenarios or drugs, and recent calculation inputs locally on the device using device storage. This local storage helps reopen prior inputs, reorder favorites, filter recents, swipe-delete entries, and clear recents.

This locally stored data is not sent to Valiora Labs by the app and is not used to create an account or cloud profile.

Information we collect

Valiora Labs does not collect personal information through PediaCalc Pro’s calculator features. The app does not include a visible login, backend account flow, cloud sync, or server-side patient record storage.

If you choose to contact us by email, request a feature, send feedback, or report an issue, we receive the email address and message content that you choose to send.

How contact information is used

Contact messages are used to respond to support requests, review feedback, evaluate feature requests, and troubleshoot reported issues. We do not sell contact information.

Third-party services

PediaCalc Pro is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may process App Store, purchase, analytics, crash, review, or device information according to Apple’s own policies and user settings.

The app can link to external evidence or reference resources. Opening external links may allow those third-party websites or services to process information under their own privacy policies.

Clinical and patient data

PediaCalc Pro is intended as a calculation support tool. Users should avoid entering personally identifying patient information into free-form contact emails unless it is necessary for support. The app’s visible workflows do not provide a backend patient record system.

Data deletion

Users can manage app data through the app’s saved and recent calculation controls, including swipe-delete and clear-all options where available. Removing the app from the device may also remove locally stored app data, subject to device and operating system behavior.

Children’s privacy

PediaCalc Pro is a clinical support calculator and is not directed to children as users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the app’s calculator features.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect app, operational, or legal changes. Updates will be posted on this page at https://pediacalcpro.valioralabs.com/privacy.html.

Contact

For privacy questions or support, contact support@valioralabs.com.
