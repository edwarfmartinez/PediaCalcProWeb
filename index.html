PediaCalc Pro is a clinical support tool, not a substitute for clinician judgment, local protocols, medication labeling, or institutional policies. Always verify calculations before treatment.

Quick emergency scenarios

The Quick tab is designed for common pediatric emergencies. Enter patient weight in kilograms or pounds and review live dose and volume calculations with safety context.

Four visible scenarios

Seizure, Anaphylaxis, Cardiac Resuscitation, and Asthma Exacerbation are shown in the Quick grid. Hypoglycemia is available in app data but is currently hidden.

Live clinical output

Each scenario can show calculated dose, volume, safety warnings, clinical context, treatment direction, repeat notes, and evidence links.

Weight-aware inputs

Numeric-limited fields update results live and help flag invalid ranges, likely pound/kilogram mistakes, and age-weight mismatches.

Drug dosing reference

Search or browse 62 drug and dose entries by medication, indication, or route. Enter patient weight and age, then expand a drug for calculation details.

  • Expanded drug cards show dose, volume, concentration, maximum dose, and warnings.
  • Max-dose caps and safety warnings are surfaced during review.
  • Neonate mode activates when age is under 28 days.
  • Neonatal-compatible drug filtering helps narrow choices for newborn patients.

Fluid calculators

Built-in calculators cover common pediatric fluid workflows with live results and warnings for clinically important boundaries.

Maintenance

Holliday-Segar 100/50/20 calculation, hourly rate, 4-2-1 detail, and 10/20/60 mL/kg bolus estimates.

Sodium correction

Weight, current sodium, target sodium, symptom severity, correction rate, fluid suggestion, estimated volume, and safe-change comparison.

Glucose infusion rate

Standard or custom dextrose concentration with infusion rate and low, typical, elevated, or high GIR interpretation.

IV drip rate

Volume, time in minutes or hours, drop factor 10/15/20/60 or custom, and final drops per minute result.

Saved work, kept local

Favorites and recents help clinicians return to common calculations without requiring an account, cloud sync, export workflow, or backend login.

Safety and usability checks

PediaCalc Pro prioritizes transparent calculations and visible warnings so the user can recognize inputs that require additional review.

  • Warnings for invalid ranges, possible unit mistakes, and age-weight mismatch.
  • Medication safeguards for maximum dose caps and neonatal context.
  • Fluid safeguards for sodium overcorrection, high GIR, and high drip rates.
  • Settings include feedback, feature requests, issue reporting, App Store review, and app version.
